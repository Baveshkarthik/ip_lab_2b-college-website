REC Chennai Placements 2023: Highest Package, Average Package & Top Recruiters

user_avater Written By Aparna Das REC Chennai Placements 2023 recorded more than 1640 students placements. 282 recruiters participated in REC Chennai Placements 2023. Some of the top recruiters of REC Chennai included Amazon, Accenture, IBM, HSBC, TCS, Cognizant etc. Earlier in the 2022 placement drive, REC Chennai highest package stood at INR 31.31 LPA & the median package stood at INR 4.5 LPA.
